Candy Rain

A Sweet Match-3

Adventure Awaits!

Immerse yourself in a deliciously fun puzzle adventure! Candy Rain is a thrilling match-3 game where you swap colorful candies, complete exciting challenges, and unlock powerful boosters. Line up jelly beans and other sweets to form rows and columns of three or more, collect bombs and power-ups, and progress through hundreds of exciting levels. Are you ready for a sugar-coated challenge?
